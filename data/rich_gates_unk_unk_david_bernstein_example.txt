On October 20, 2017, the Acting Attorney General confirmed in a memorandum the Special Counsel's investigative authority as to several individuals and entities. First, as part of a full and thorough investigation of the Russian government's efforts to interfere in the 2016 presidential election," the Special Counsel was authorized to investigate the pertinent activities of Michael Cohen, Richard Gates, unk unk, Roger Stone, and another person." "Confirmation of the authorization to investigate such individuals", the memorandum stressed, "does not suggest that the Special Counsel" has made a determinateion that any of them has committed a crime.
